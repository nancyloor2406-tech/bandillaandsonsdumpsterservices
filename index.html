Frequently Asked Questions

1. How fast can you deliver a dumpster in major cities?

In Houston, Miami, Phoenix, Atlanta, and Dallas, we typically deliver within 24–48 hours. Same-day service is sometimes available if you call before 10 a.m.

2. What can’t I put in the dumpster?

Hazardous materials (paint, chemicals, batteries), tires, asbestos, medical waste, and propane tanks are prohibited. We provide a full list at booking to avoid landfill issues.

3. Do I need a permit for my driveway in Chicago or Denver?

No—if the bin stays on your private property. Permits are only required if it blocks a public street or sidewalk, which varies by city (e.g., San Diego, Charlotte, Columbus).

4. What’s included in the price?

Delivery, pickup, 7–10 day rental, and a standard weight allowance (varies by bin size and location). All fees are disclosed upfront—no fuel surcharges or admin markups.

5. Can I get a bin for just one day in Las Vegas or Nashville?

Yes. We offer 24-hour rentals for fast cleanouts—ideal for move-outs, small demos, or event cleanup.

6. How do I choose the right size for a kitchen demo in Seattle?

A 20 yard dumpster typically handles a full kitchen remodel. For smaller jobs (cabinets only), a 10 or 15 yard may suffice. We’ll help you decide.
